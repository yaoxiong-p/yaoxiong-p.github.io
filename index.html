Research Interests

My research focuses on designing smart systems that adapt, respond, and interact with their environment. By integrating computer modeling and simulation with close collaboration with experimental scientists, I aim to create systems that learn from nature and address pressing challenges in technology and healthcare. My work advances computational methods, nanomaterials, polymer physics, clean energy, flexible electronics, soft robotics, and biomedical technologies, such as drug delivery and medical devices.
