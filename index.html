Research Interests

My research focuses on designing smart materials that adapt, respond, and interact with their environment. By integrating soft matter physics and multiscale modeling with close collaboration with experimental scientists, I aim to create systems that learn from nature and address pressing challenges in organoid engineering, regenerative medicine, and sustainable materials. My work advances molecular engineering, computational methods, nanomaterials, polymer physics, clean energy, flexible electronics, soft robotics, and biomedical technologies, such as drug delivery and medical devices.
