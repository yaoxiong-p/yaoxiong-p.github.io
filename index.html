Research Interests

My research focuses on designing smart materials that adapt, respond, and interact with their environment. By integrating soft matter physics and multiscale modeling with close collaboration with experimental scientists, I aim to create systems that learn from nature and address pressing challenges in organoid engineering, regenerative medicine, and sustainable materials. My work advances molecular engineering, multiscale computational methods, polymer physics, adaptive soft materials, soft robotics, and biomedical technologies, including drug delivery and organoid engineering, supported by strong interdisciplinary collaborations and a commitment to inclusive, hands-on education.
