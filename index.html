Research Interests

My research focuses on designing and understanding adaptive, active, and responsive materials through the integration of advanced computational modeling and experimental collaboration. I develop and apply multiscale simulation approaches, including field theoretic simulations (FTS), dissipative particle dynamics (DPD), and continuum modeling such as finite element method (FEM), to uncover the governing principles that guide the development of next-generation functional materials. These efforts aim to accelerate innovation in electronics, iontronics, sustainable energy systems, soft robotics, and biomedical technologies including drug delivery and medical devices, ultimately connecting theory with experiment to enable transformative applications.
